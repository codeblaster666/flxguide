## Prioritization Criteria

When moving items from backlog to sprint, consider:

1. **Impact**: How much does this move the needle?
2. **Effort**: How much work is required?
3. **Dependencies**: What needs to happen first?
4. **Timing**: Is there seasonal relevance?
5. **Revenue Potential**: Does this support monetization?

## Wine Trails

### Seneca Lake Wine Trail
- Largest wine trail in the region
- 30+ member wineries
- Focus: Full-bodied reds, dry Rieslings

### Cayuga Lake Wine Trail
- America's first wine trail (est. 1983)
- 15+ member wineries
- Focus: Diverse varietals, farm wineries

### Keuka Lake Wine Trail
- Scenic Y-shaped lake
- 10+ member wineries
- Focus: Traditional methods, small producers

### Canandaigua Lake Wine Trail
- Growing trail
- 8+ member wineries
- Focus: Boutique wineries

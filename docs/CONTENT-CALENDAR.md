## Style Guidelines

### Voice and Tone
- Knowledgeable but approachable
- Local expertise, not tourist fluff
- Practical and actionable
- Honest reviews (including negatives when warranted)

### SEO Requirements
- Target keyword in title and H1
- Meta description under 160 characters
- At least one internal link
- Alt text on all images
- Target 1,500+ words for guides

### Image Requirements
- Featured image required
- Minimum 3 images per article
- Original photography preferred
- Proper attribution for stock/licensed images

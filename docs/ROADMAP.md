# FLXguide.com - Project Roadmap

## Overview

This document outlines the phased approach to building FLXguide.com from initial research through monetization.

---

## Phase 1: Research
**Duration: 2-4 weeks**

### Objectives
- Understand the competitive landscape
- Identify content gaps and opportunities
- Define target audience personas
- Gather foundational data for all content verticals

### Key Deliverables
- [ ] Complete competitor analysis
- [ ] Deep dive research on all 6 content verticals
- [ ] Audience persona documentation
- [ ] SEO keyword research
- [ ] Content gap analysis
- [ ] Technical requirements document

### Research Areas
| Vertical | Priority | Status |
|----------|----------|--------|
| Wine | High | Not Started |
| Fishing | High | Not Started |
| Hunting | Medium | Not Started |
| Camping | Medium | Not Started |
| Restaurants | Medium | Not Started |
| State Parks | Medium | Not Started |

### Success Criteria
- All research documents completed and reviewed
- Clear understanding of market opportunity
- Technical stack decision made
- Go/no-go decision for MVP phase

---

## Phase 2: MVP Launch
**Duration: 1-2 months**

### Objectives
- Launch minimum viable website
- Establish brand presence
- Begin building SEO authority
- Deploy first interactive tool (Wine Tour App)

### Key Deliverables
- [ ] Domain and hosting configured
- [ ] Basic site architecture implemented
- [ ] 10-20 foundational content pieces published
- [ ] Wine Tour App v1.0 deployed
- [ ] Analytics and tracking setup
- [ ] Basic SEO optimization

### MVP Features
| Feature | Priority | Complexity |
|---------|----------|------------|
| Homepage | Critical | Low |
| Wine section landing | Critical | Low |
| Wine Tour App | Critical | High |
| Fishing section landing | High | Low |
| About/Contact pages | Medium | Low |
| Newsletter signup | Medium | Low |

### Success Criteria
- Site live and indexed by Google
- Wine Tour App functional and usable
- Baseline traffic metrics established
- No critical bugs or performance issues

---

## Phase 3: Content Building
**Duration: Ongoing**

### Objectives
- Build comprehensive content library
- Establish topical authority
- Grow organic traffic
- Develop content production workflows

### Content Targets
| Quarter | Articles | Guides | Tools |
|---------|----------|--------|-------|
| Q1 | 20 | 4 | 1 |
| Q2 | 30 | 6 | 1 |
| Q3 | 40 | 8 | 2 |
| Q4 | 50 | 10 | 2 |

### Content Types
- **Articles**: News, seasonal updates, event coverage
- **Guides**: Comprehensive how-to and destination guides
- **Tools**: Interactive applications and calculators
- **Lists**: Top 10s, best-of compilations
- **Reviews**: Wineries, restaurants, campgrounds

### Success Criteria
- Consistent publishing schedule maintained
- Month-over-month traffic growth
- Content quality metrics (time on page, bounce rate)
- Growing backlink profile

---

## Phase 4: Tool Expansion
**Duration: 3-6 months (after MVP)**

### Objectives
- Differentiate from competitors through interactive tools
- Increase user engagement and return visits
- Create shareable, linkable assets

### Planned Tools
| Tool | Description | Priority |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Wine Tour Planner | Route optimization, winery info | Complete |
| Fishing Spot Finder | Interactive lake maps, species data | High |
| Hunting Season Calendar | Regulations, dates, license info | High |
| Campground Finder | Availability, amenities, booking links | Medium |
| Restaurant Finder | Filters, reviews, reservations | Medium |
| Trail Guide | Interactive park maps, difficulty ratings | Low |

### Technical Considerations
- Mobile-first design
- Offline functionality where applicable
- API integrations (weather, booking systems)
- User accounts for saved preferences

### Success Criteria
- At least 3 tools deployed
- Measurable engagement metrics per tool
- User feedback incorporated
- Tools cited/linked by other sites

---

## Phase 5: Monetization
**Duration: 6-12 months (from launch)**

### Objectives
- Achieve sustainable revenue
- Diversify income streams
- Maintain content quality and user trust

### Revenue Streams
| Stream | Timeline | Projected % of Revenue |
|--------|----------|----------------------|
| Display Ads (AdSense/Mediavine) | Month 3+ | 40% |
| Affiliate Marketing | Month 1+ | 30% |
| Sponsored Content | Month 6+ | 15% |
| Premium Tools/Features | Month 9+ | 10% |
| Local Business Partnerships | Month 6+ | 5% |

### Monetization Milestones
- [ ] 10,000 monthly pageviews - Apply for premium ad networks
- [ ] 25,000 monthly pageviews - Mediavine application
- [ ] 50,000 monthly pageviews - Direct ad sales consideration
- [ ] 100,000 monthly pageviews - Premium feature launch

### Affiliate Opportunities
- Wine club signups
- Fishing/hunting gear (Amazon, Bass Pro)
- Camping equipment
- Restaurant reservations (OpenTable)
- Hotel/lodging bookings
- Tour and experience bookings

### Success Criteria
- Positive ROI on hosting/development costs
- Multiple active revenue streams
- Revenue growth month-over-month
- User experience not degraded by monetization

## Risk Assessment

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Competition from established sites | High | Medium | Focus on tools and local expertise |
| Content quality inconsistency | Medium | High | Develop style guide and review process |
| Technical issues with tools | Medium | Medium | Thorough testing, staged rollouts |
| SEO algorithm changes | Medium | High | Diversify traffic sources |
| Seasonal traffic fluctuations | High | Medium | Develop off-season content strategy |
